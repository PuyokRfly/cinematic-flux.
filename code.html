Mission Support

Everything you need to know about Cinematic Flux

How do I save to my USB?

Cinematic Flux is designed to work with your hardware. Ensure your USB is mounted, then set its path in your .env file.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use kernel-level os.sync() triggers after every operation.

What formats are supported?

We support high-definition MP4 videos and high-bitrate MP3 audio.
